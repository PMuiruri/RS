About Me

My name is Rachael. I am a registered nurse working in Accident and Emergency in Finland. I graduated in 2005 from JAMK University College of Health and Social Sciences.

Over the years, I have worked in various departments including elderly homes, geriatric wards, medical wards, emergency wards, cardiac wards, and mainly Accident and Emergency.

I have learned Finnish both through courses and self-study and hold certificates from JAMK and Helsinki University. I teach healthcare Finnish to health professionals who have completed basic Finnish courses (B2, C1, C2).
